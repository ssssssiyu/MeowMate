MeowMate Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

1. Information Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Cat information you provide (name, breed, age, etc.)
  • Cat photos you upload
  • Device information (to provide better service)

2. Information Usage

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide cat health management services
  • Send important reminders and notifications
  • Improve our services

3. Information Protection

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • Use encryption technology to protect data transmission
  • Regularly update security measures
  • Limit employee access to user information

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (for data storage and analytics)
  • The Cat API (for cat breed information)
  • OpenAI (for health advice)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.
